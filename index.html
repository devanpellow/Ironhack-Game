Somewhat bad news, Earth is under attack from an alien invasion.
To make matters worse, you're the one chosen to save us from total annihilation.

Your mission:

  • Shoot down alien ships while dodging falling debris from their mothership.
  • Avoid the debris at all costs. We didn't have the budget to give you a strong enough ship.
  • Don't let three aliens reach Earth or else they will be too strong to stop.

No pressure though, it's only the fate of the world.

Controls

Fly Up: ARROW UP

Fly Down: ARROW DOWN

Fly Right: ARROW RIGHT

Fly Left: ARROW LEFT

Shoot: SPACE BAR
